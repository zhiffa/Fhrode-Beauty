GET THE LOOK : ALEXA DEMIE!

Alexa Demie's makeup signature is a captivating blend of 90s glam and modern edge. Think sharp cat eyes, a bold lip (often a nude with a brown gloss for a dimensional effect), and a flawless base with glowing skin. She loves experimenting with pops of color on the eyes, while keeping the brows natural and brushed-up. Whether it's a red carpet look or a casual day out, her makeup always exudes confidence and a touch of vintage Hollywood allure.


MAKEUP GUIDE

High coverage makeup : YSL FOUNDation, lip : mac lip pencil, fenty beauty gloss bomb, nude rough dior proof lipstick, eyeliner : stilla stay all day liquid liner. mascara : volumizing too faced. eyebrow : anastasia beverly hills pomade
